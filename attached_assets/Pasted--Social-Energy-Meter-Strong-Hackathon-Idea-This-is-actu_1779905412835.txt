# Social Energy Meter — Strong Hackathon Idea

This is actually a very good choice for this hackathon because it’s:

* personal
* emotionally intelligent
* AI-enhanced
* visually demoable
* easy to explain in 10 seconds

It feels like a **real product**, not just “AI wrapper #284”.

---

# Core Concept

## Problem

People track:

* steps
* sleep
* calories
* productivity

…but nobody tracks:

# social exhaustion.

Some interactions energize us.
Some drain us.

Your app helps users understand:

* who gives energy
* what situations cause burnout
* when to recharge
* how social patterns affect mood

---

# MVP (What You Can Actually Finish)

Keep scope SMALL.

## Main Flow

### User logs:

* who they met
* duration
* mood before
* mood after
* interaction type

Examples:

* work meeting
* friend hangout
* family call
* party
* texting

---

# AI Features

## 1. Energy Analysis

AI notices patterns like:

> “Group settings after 7 PM tend to drain you.”

or

> “Conversations with close friends improve your mood.”

---

## 2. Burnout Prediction

Example:

> “You’ve had 11 social interactions in 3 days. High exhaustion risk.”

---

## 3. Recovery Suggestions

Example:

* quiet evening
* music
* solo walk
* journaling

---

# Best Feature (Very Important)

## “Energy Map”

Visual graph showing:

* people who energize
* people who drain
* neutral interactions

This is your demo-video “wow moment”.
